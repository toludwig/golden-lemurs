Abstract

When approaching this competition, we decided early on that various forms of neural networks could be an interesting and powerful solution to the task. We proceeded to seek out state-of-the-art designs of various kinds of neural networks fit to the various forms of data we could extract and developed classifiers building on that. While some of these classifiers did not fulfill our expectations, we observed great results with convolutional networks applied to word embeddings of READMEs and commit messages. On top of this, we used a deep feed-forward neural classifier to combine the predictions of our sub-nets into a final conclusion instead of using a linear classifier.
